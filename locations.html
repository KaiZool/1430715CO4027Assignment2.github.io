Endor

Climate: Tempearate
Primary Terrain: Forests, Mountains, Deserts, Savannas, Lakes and Rivers

Endor (also known as the Forest Moon of Endor and the Sanctuary Moon) was a small forested moon orbiting the gas giant planet of Endor and was the farthest moon away from it. An enchanted world, Endor was notable for being the native home of the Ewoks, and being the location of the 'Battle of Endor', the climatic confrontation between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance.

Tatooine

Climate: Arid
Primary Terrain : Desert, Some Oases, Canyons

A desert world, Tatooine would play a key role in the flilms, being the starting point of the franchise and home planet to the protagonist, Luke Skywalker. Taking place a year after the previous film, Luke, Leia, Chewbacca, Lando, C-3PO and R2-D2, travel to Tatooine to infiltrate Jabba's palace and rescue their comrade, Han Solo, from the notorius crime lord, Jabba The Hutt.

Dagobah

Climate: Murky
Primary Terrain: Swamp, Bogs, Jungle

Dagobah, an isolated planet overun with swamps and forests, but is also said to have a very strong connection to the force, became home to the great Jedi Master, Yoda, after his self-imposed Exile. During the course of the films, Dagobah plays an important role as it is where Luke Skywalker encounters the old Jedi Master and receives his training to become a full-fledged Jedi.

Death Star II

After the destruction of the first Death Star, a second one was immedately under construction by the Galactic Empire. Death Star II, was iconic, for the fact that it is the place of climatic confrontation between the Jedi and Sith.
