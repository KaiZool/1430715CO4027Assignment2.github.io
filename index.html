Main Stars: Carrie Fisher, Mark Hamill and Harisson Ford
Date Released: 25 May 1983
Genre: Action, Advemture, Fantasy and Sci-Fi


SYNOPSIS:

Taking place one year after the last film (Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back). The protagonists, Luke Skywalker and team, travel to Tatooine to rescue their companion, Han Solo from the crime lord Jabba The Hutt.

Star Ships will clash and lightsabers will duel in the epic conclusion to the original trilogy

As the Galactic Empire prepares to crush the Rebel Alliance with the new-and-improved Death Star II. Luke Skywalker will now have to face both Darth Vader and Darth Sidious in a climatic confrontation between good and evil.
